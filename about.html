About Bella Notte

Our story of passion, tradition, and authentic Italian cuisine

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Marco Antonelli, Bella Notte brings the authentic flavors of Tuscany to the heart of Mumbai. Our journey began with a simple dream - to share the joy of traditional Italian cuisine with food lovers everywhere.

Every dish we serve tells a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and love. From our handmade pasta prepared fresh each morning to our wood-fired pizzas cooked in our imported Italian brick oven, we use only the finest ingredients, many sourced directly from Italy, to ensure an authentic dining experience that transports you straight to the cobblestone streets of Florence.

What started as a small family restaurant has grown into Mumbai's most beloved Italian dining destination. Yet we've never forgotten our roots - every recipe comes from Chef Marco's grandmother's kitchen, every technique honored through generations of Italian tradition, and every meal served with the warmth and hospitality that defines true Italian culture.

Today, Bella Notte has become more than just a restaurant - it's a place where families celebrate milestones, couples fall in love, and friends create memories that last a lifetime. We're honored to be part of your special moments and committed to making every visit an unforgettable journey through the flavors of Italy.

Our warm, inviting dining room where memories are made

Head Chef & Owner

Chef Marco Antonelli

Executive Chef & Founder

Born in the rolling hills of Tuscany, Chef Marco Antonelli learned the art of cooking from his nonna in her rustic kitchen overlooking the vineyards of Chianti. His passion for authentic Italian cuisine led him to train at the prestigious Culinary Institute of Florence and work in Michelin-starred restaurants across Italy.

In 2015, Marco brought his culinary expertise and family recipes to Mumbai, creating Bella Notte as a tribute to his Italian heritage. His commitment to using traditional techniques and authentic ingredients has earned the restaurant numerous awards and a loyal following of food enthusiasts.

"Cooking is not just about following recipes," says Chef Marco. "It's about sharing love, creating connections, and bringing people together around the table. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries a piece of my heart and my homeland."

Our Values & Philosophy

Fresh & Authentic

We source the finest ingredients daily, importing specialty items from Italy to ensure authenticity in every dish. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Family Tradition

Every recipe comes from generations of Italian family tradition. We honor our heritage while creating new memories for your family.

Warm Hospitality

Italian hospitality is legendary. We treat every guest like family, ensuring your dining experience is memorable and heartwarming.

Community Focus

We're proud to be part of Mumbai's culinary landscape, supporting local suppliers and creating jobs in our community.

Excellence

We never settle for good enough. Every dish, every service interaction, every detail is crafted to exceed your expectations.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, from sourcing to waste reduction, ensuring a better future for generations to come.
